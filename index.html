AI Ping Pong Game







Created By Ashika Karuppaiah

Instructions

1. Keep your laptop screen staright.

2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop.

3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decreasing manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.

4. Now press the play button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the computer.

5. The red paddle is your paddle, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
